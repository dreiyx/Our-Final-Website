Do you offer mobility amenities at your resorts?

Sandals strives to make your vacation a memorable experience no matter what the degree of your mobility disability. Our accommodating staff members are happy to assist you with any necessary information and do everything to help you achieve total relaxation while on your beach vacation. It is important to let your travel agent or Sandals reservations agent know what your specific mobility issue is and the special services that may be needed. Some of our resorts can accommodate those with mobility issues; however, due to the nature and geography of our resorts, there are instances where mobility accommodation may be limited depending on your specific need. We will note your special requirements and make every effort to accommodate them. Additionally, some of our resorts offer beach wheelchairs, ramps, and lifts for convenient access to the beach.
